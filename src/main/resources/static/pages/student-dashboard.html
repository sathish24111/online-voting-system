Student Voting Guidelines

  • You must log in using your Roll Number and Password.
  • A 6-digit OTP code is sent to your registered Gmail address to verify your identity.
  • You can only submit your ballot **once**. Double voting is blocked by the system database.
  • True Ballot Secrecy: Your vote selections are stored anonymously, leaving no record matching you to your candidate choices.
  • Results will be compiled and published only after the election ends.
