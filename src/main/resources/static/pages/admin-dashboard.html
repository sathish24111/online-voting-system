Welcome to the Administrator Panel

From this console, you can perform structural changes and audits of university elections. Follow these standard workflows to execute the voting process:

1
Import Voters Directory

Visit "Manage Students" to import clean .xlsx or .csv student spreadsheets. Duplicates are auto-skipped.

2
Register Candidates

Visit "Manage Candidates" to register candidates, assign their positions, and upload manifestos & profiles.

3
Manage Election Status

Use "Election Settings" to schedule start/end dates and toggle election states (Start, Pause, End).
